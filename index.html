🏫 RTE Act — Free Private School Admission (Critical)

Every private school in Chhattisgarh must reserve 25% seats for ST/EWS children. FREE education from Class 1 to Class 8. School cannot charge any fees. Government reimburses the school. Applications at rte.cg.nic.in — usually open January to March each year.

Forest Rights Act 2006 — Your Land Title is a Legal Right

Every ST family cultivating forest land since before December 2005 has a legal right to a land title (patta). CG has 45% forest cover — yet nearly 50% of FRA claims have been rejected. If your claim was rejected, you can re-file. The portal guides you step by step.
